Why $HNGOTR?

HungryOtter is a secure, deflationary token that is charity-focused and designed to reward long time holders. This project is by the community and 100% for the community.

We believe in giving back. This means that for every transaction performed, 2.5% of the fees incurred will be send back to the charity pool. Holders of HungryOtter can be proud to back a project that supports a good cause!
